Woods elementary the blue ribbon school

Woods Elementary School is a highly rated, public school located in Fort Smith, AR. It has 595 students in grades PK, K-6 with a student-teacher ratio of 19 to 1. According to state test scores, 82% of students are at least proficient in math and 71% in reading.
